Motivation

This week at EDA we were asked to read Chapter 6 of 'Search Inside Yourself' by Chade-Meng Tan.

Pleasure, Passion and Purpose

One of the topics this chapter talks about is describing various types of happiness. If we are looking through a life lens, the different types could be described as follows:

  1. Pleasure - Pleasure is that "instant satisfaction" feeling. It's like when you've just had a beer at the end of a hard day's work, or you've just been to a concert of your favourite band. It can be described as a party experience and is often short lived.
  2. Passion - Passion can be desribed as "flow", or "being in the zone". It's about being absorted in what you're doing and time just seems to fly by.
  3. Higher Purpose - Which is about being part of something that is bigger than yourself and which has meaning to you. It's like joining a charitable organisation, or spending time with family.

Discovering Values and Higher Purpose

Some of my core values include spending time wisely, developing lasting relationships and contributing to something meaningful.

Some of the things i stand up for is encouraging others to perform at the best of their abilities and standing up for what is right and morally just.
