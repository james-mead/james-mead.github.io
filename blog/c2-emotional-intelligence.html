Emotional Intelligence

I have just finished reading the first chapter of Search Inside Yourself which is a book written by Chade-Meng Tan who is a Google Engineer. Below are some of my learnings.

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to perceive, evaluate and manage emotions and is a technique that is developed through mindfulness and meditation and is one of the best predictors of success at work and fulfillment in life. Benefits include:

  1. Stellar work performance
  2. Outstanding leadership
  3. The ability to create the conditions for happiness

It can be broken down in to the following domains:

  1. Self-awareness: Knowledge of one's internal states, preferences, resources, and intuitions
  2. Self-regulation: Management of one's internal states, impulses, and resources
  3. Motivation: Emotional tendencies that guide or facilitate reaching goals
  4. Empathy: Awareness of other' feelings, needs and concerns
  5. Social Skills: Adeptness at inducing desirable responses in others

Stellar Work Performance

Studies have shown that Emotional Intelligence is twice as important for excellence as pure intellect and expertise.

Outstanding Leadership

Studies show Emotional Intelligence make better people leaders and are often attributed to creating better work environements with the ability to use their interpersonal skills to better interact with co-workers.

The Ability To Create The Conditions For Happiness

Emotional Intelligence enable us to use skills that help create conditions for happiness. Happiness is a skill that can trained.

Two minutes of mindfulness

I found that using the affect labeling technique meant i could label how i was feeling at a specific point in time and studies have shown this to be effective at lessening the impact it has on our emotional well-being.
